Plushy Pals - iPhone game. Jan 2013 to May 2013.

During my last semester in college, I made a fun iPhone game with two friends. It is kinda like the popular running game Temple Run, but with cuter characters. Thanks to the awesome artwork from Linh Tran Do, we have different maps featuring jungle, mountain, dessert and various characters including a monkey, a pig, a bear. Game play is simple: turn left/right and jump to avoid hitting walls or obstacles; collect items to get bonus points and unlock new maps; the goal is to get as many points as possible before you hit something and die.

We didn't have the chance to put it in app store but here is the trailer. You can also see the source code in my github.

Inspira - Microsoft Imagine Cup 2011 Entry. Oct 2010 - Dec 2010

During Grace Hopper Celebration in 2010, I learned about Microsoft Imagine Cup, a global student technology competition. The theme that year was to solve the world's tough problem. Me and two partners chose to focus on the lack female in the technology field. Back then the topic wasn't as hot as now and there weren't many good ways to connect women in the technology field. We decided to start with college students and build an windows phone app(it's a Microsoft sponsored competition) that connects female engineering students to mentors and other peers.

Here is our competition video entry. We didn't go very far, but definitely a very good learning experience. It was my first time working sorta like a project manager as well as the main developer.
